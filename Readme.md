# 🌱 AgroMind

> **AI-Powered Smart Crop Management & Disease Prediction System**

AgroMind is an intelligent farming assistant designed to help farmers detect crop diseases, predict potential risks, and receive personalized treatment recommendations using Artificial Intelligence. Our goal is to improve crop health, increase productivity, and support sustainable farming practices.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🌿 AI-powered Crop Disease Detection
- 📷 Image-based Disease Identification
- 💊 Smart Treatment Recommendations
- 🌦️ Weather-based Disease Prediction
- 📊 Crop Health Dashboard
- 🗓️ Personalized Crop Care Planner
- 🎙️ Multilingual & Voice Assistant
- 📈 Farm Health Analytics
- 📍 Nearby Agriculture Centers & Experts
- 📄 Downloadable Crop Health Reports

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers often struggle to identify crop diseases at an early stage, leading to reduced yields and financial losses.

AgroMind leverages AI to provide early disease detection, predictive insights, and actionable recommendations, enabling farmers to make informed decisions and improve agricultural outcomes.
